About the Studio

Dukun Strike is a pioneering game studio with a passion for storytelling and innovation. Founded by a team of dedicated game developers, artists, and storytellers, our studio is committed to creating games that break the mold. Our roots in Indonesian culture and mythology give us a unique perspective, allowing us to craft games that are not just fun but also culturally significant.

  • Our Vision: To become a leading force in the gaming industry by merging traditional folklore with cutting-edge technology, creating games that resonate on a global scale.

  • Our Mission: To craft unforgettable experiences that connect players with the rich stories of the past while challenging them with the innovations of the future.

  • Our Team: A diverse group of creators united by a shared love of gaming and storytelling, each member brings unique skills to the table, helping us create games that are works of art.

  • Why We Stand Out: We don’t just follow trends; we set them. By blending ancient myths with modern gameplay mechanics, we create games that stand out in a crowded market.
