Our History

2010

The Beginning

Dukun Strike was founded by a group of passionate developers with a shared vision: to create a game that captures the rich and mystical folklore of Indonesia. Starting as a small indie studio, the team worked tirelessly to bring traditional stories to life through interactive gameplay.

2012

First Release: Dukun Strike

The studio released its first game, "Dukun Strike," which quickly gained popularity for its unique blend of strategy and mythological elements. The game featured rich storylines inspired by Indonesian legends, captivating players worldwide.

2013

Recognition and Awards

The studio received several awards for game design and storytelling, solidifying its reputation in the gaming industry. Dukun Strike was celebrated for its innovative approach, blending cultural heritage with engaging gameplay.

2015

Expanding Horizons
